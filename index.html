Get started
with your new Trezór

Download the Trezór Suite app and follow the steps to get started.

This is how it works

1

Download Trezór Suite

Download Trezór Suite for desktop for the best experience, or click "Continue in browser" to open the web app.

2

Install the app

Install the desktop app on your computer. This step isn't necessary for web app users.

3

Set up your Trezór

Plug in your device and follow the steps to set up your Trezór hardware wallet. This takes about 15 minutes.

4

Move your coins

You're ready to protect your crypto securely. Check our guide to learn how to transfer your assets from an exchange to your Trezór.

What is Trezór Suite?

Trezór Suite is our comprehensive desktop application that provides:

  • Complete control over your cryptocurrencies
  • Direct integration with your Trezór hardware wallet
  • Built-in exchange functionality
  • Real-time portfolio tracking
  • Enhanced security features

Why should I choose Trezór Suite over other wallets?

Trezór Suite offers unique advantages:

  • Seamless hardware wallet integration
  • User-friendly interface designed for both beginners and experts
  • Regular security updates and improvements
  • Support for multiple cryptocurrencies
  • Advanced transaction features

How secure is Trezór Suite?

Trezór Suite implements multiple security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all operations
  • Offline transaction signing
  • Regular security audits
  • No private key exposure to the internet
  • Protection against phishing attacks

What cryptocurrencies does Trezór Suite support?

Trezór Suite supports a wide range of cryptocurrencies:

  • Bitcoin (BTC) and all major Bitcoin forks
  • Ethereum (ETH) and ERC-20 tokens
  • Litecoin (LTC), Dogecoin (DOGE)
  • Cardano (ADA), Ripple (XRP)
  • And many more, with regular updates adding new coins
